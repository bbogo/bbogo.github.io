Research Interests:

  • Calculus of variations, shape optimization
  • Numerical shape optimization: $\Gamma$-convergence methods, relaxation, phase field, fundamental solutions
  • Shape optimization for eigenvalue problems: Laplace operator, Steklov spectrum
  • Optimal partitioning problems
