Waves - a shape optimization approach

(work in progress, joint with Dorin Bucur)

Here are some initial numerical computations for the problem $$ \min_{\int_D u=c} \frac{\mu^2}{\int_D |\nabla u|^2}+g\int_D yu +c \left( \varepsilon \int_D |\nabla u|^2 +\frac{1}{\varepsilon} \int_D u^2(1-u)^2\right) $$ It is supposed that solutions to this problem are critical waves.
