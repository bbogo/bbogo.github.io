Numerical study of the Ohta Kawasaki functional

Dyblock copolymers contain two chains of similar monomers $A$ and $B$ with a strong chemical bond. A dyblock copolymer melt can contain uniform distributions of the two types of molecules. Below a certain temperature the segregation of similar monomers occurs. This segregation cannot occur at macroscopic scale since the chemical bonds linking the copolymer cannot be easily broken. Thus segregation only occurs at a mesoscopic scale and periodic structures occur.
